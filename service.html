Our Services

Career Programme & Pre-Recruitment Training

At the Royal Institute of Cruise and Hospitality, we provide career training and international placement opportunities aboard cruise ships.

We are currently offering urgent openings for hotel management professionals and photographers. Our programs are aligned with international cruise industry standards to prepare you for global careers at sea.

Vacancies include:

  • CDP, Commi I, Commi II, Commi III (Hot & Cold)
  • Bakery, Pastry, Pantry, Butcher, Kitchen Cleaners
  • Veg & Fruit Carver, Hotel Store Keepers
  • Housekeeping Assistants (Male & Female)
  • F&B Service: Waiter, Waitress, Utility, Bar Tender, Bar Server
  • Bar Utility, Stage Staff, Photographers
  • Light & Sound Engineers, Fitness Trainers
  • Hair Dressers, Spa Therapist (Female only)
  • Jewellery & Diamond Watch Sales Executives (Female only)

* Minimum 2 years experience and fluency in English are required for all positions.
