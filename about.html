About Royal Institute of Cruise & Hospitality

Crafting Careers Since 2014

Established in 2014, the Royal Institute of Cruise and Hospitality (RICH) has been a beacon of quality training and international job placements.

We offer American Culinary Federation based syllabus tailored to meet global hospitality standards. Our training focuses on hands-on experience with pre-recruitment programs specially curated for cruise ship employment.

With 300+ successful student placements in leading international cruise companies, we take pride in helping individuals build exciting careers on the seas!

Located at Kaloor, Thanikalpara Road, our institute is easily accessible and offers world-class training facilities.

Eligibility: Diploma or Degree holders with minimum 2 years of work experience. Fluency in English is mandatory.
